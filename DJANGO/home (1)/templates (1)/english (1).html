Our story

Lying in Märkischer Kreis, South-Westphalia, at the right bank of the Lenne, Gästehaus Altena is a place where you forget about time. Established in 2019, from the ruins of an old nursing home, it has been completely rebuilt and now it perfectly represents the mix of traditional and modern spirit - a well-known picture here in Northern Germany.

The old beauty

A few minutes away from us lies a breathtaking architectural wonder, Burgaltena - the Altena castle.This medieval beauty sleeps on top of Klusenberg hill and watches over the town for centuries.
